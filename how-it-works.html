How Harlo Unkle Works

4 simple steps. One call. Your home repair sorted. No middleman markups — you pay the provider's rate directly.

Step by Step

From Problem to Sorted in 4 Steps

1

Call or WhatsApp Us

Describe your problem. Send a photo if you can — this helps us give an accurate quote before anyone arrives. Works for everything from a dripping tap to a burst pipe, a flickering light to a full power trip.

2

We Route You to the Nearest Vetted Provider

Based on your location (HDB town or condo) and the type of work needed, we connect you with the closest qualified provider in our network. No long-distance travel surcharges. We cover all 29 URA planning areas across Singapore.

3

Provider Gives You a Transparent Quote

You get a firm price before any work starts. If the job scope changes on-site, the provider must get your agreement on the new price first. We enforce this — hidden fees are the #1 complaint from Singapore homeowners, and we don't tolerate it.

4

Job Done. You Pay the Provider Directly.

No middleman pricing. You pay the provider's quoted rate, not ours. Our platform is free for homeowners. We earn through our platform subscription with providers, not by inflating your bill.
