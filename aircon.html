FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my aircon in Singapore?

At least once every 3–4 months for general wash. Chemical wash is recommended every 12–18 months. More frequent if you run it daily.

How much does aircon servicing cost?

General wash starts from $40–$55 per unit. Chemical wash is $80–$120 per unit. Gas top-up ranges from $60–$150.

Why is my aircon leaking water?

Usually a blocked drainage pipe or dirty evaporator coil. A quick service typically resolves it.

How long does servicing take?

A general wash takes 30-45 minutes. Chemical wash takes 45-60 minutes.

Do you service all aircon brands?

Yes — Mitsubishi, Daikin, Panasonic, York, LG, Carrier, Midea, and all other common brands in Singapore.
