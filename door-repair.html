Frequently Asked Questions

How much does door repair cost?

Simple adjustments start at $50–$80. Lock replacements or full alignment work is $80–$200.

How fast can a technician arrive?

Most offer same-day service. For emergencies like lockouts, some arrive in 1–2 hours.

Should I repair or replace the whole door?

If the frame is solid, repair is much cheaper. If it's water-damaged, a swap might be needed.
