FAQ

Common Questions

My HDB door is sagging and won't close — can it be fixed?
Yes — most HDB door sagging is caused by loose hinges or worn floor. Realigning and adjusting hinges costs $50–$80. If the floor is worn, planing may be needed.
What digital locks do you install?
Samsung, Yale, Gateman, Philips, Kaadas — all HDB/con approved models. Installation from $80. We can also supply the lock.
Do you repair HDB iron gates?
Yes — gate weld repair, roller latch replacement, door closer install, anti-lift device. From $100.
How much does a door closer cost?
Door closer supply and install starts at $60–$120 depending on the door weight and closer grade.
I lost my digital lock key card — can you help?
Most digital locks can be reset with the admin code. If you're locked out, we can route you to an emergency locksmith.
