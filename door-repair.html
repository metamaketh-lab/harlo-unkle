Frequently Asked Questions — Door Repair Singapore

How much does door repair cost in Singapore?

Simple hinge or lock adjustments start from $50–$80. Full door lock replacements or alignment work can range from $80–$200 depending on material and hardware.

How quickly can a repair technician arrive?

Most providers offer same-day service. For emergencies (e.g., locked out of your room or main door), some arrive within 1–2 hours.

Do you cover all Singapore HDB, Condo and Landed types?

Yes. Our network covers all HDB towns (Tampines, Jurong, Woodlands, Sengkang, Bedok, Bishan, Ang Mo Kio, etc.), Condo properties, and Landed homes across Singapore.

What door brands/types do you service?

All major types: HDB standard doors, security gates, wooden panel doors, sliding glass doors, metal frame doors, and glass entrance doors.

Should I repair or replace the whole door?

If the frame is intact and it's just hinges or locks, repair is much cheaper ($50–$150 vs $500+ for a full replacement). If the door is severely warped or water-damaged, a full swap may be needed.

Can I get a quote before the repair starts?

Yes! All our recommended providers offer free, no-obligation quotes before any work begins.
