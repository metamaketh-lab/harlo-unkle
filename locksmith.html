FAQ

Common Questions

How much does an emergency locksmith cost in Singapore at 2am?
Standard HDB door unlock costs $80–$150. Some firms charge fixed rates day and night, while others add a 50–200% after-hours surcharge. Always ask for a firm quote before they arrive.
Do I need a fire-rated lock for my HDB main door?
Yes — if your main door is within 3m of the staircase or lift lobby, SCDF requires a 30-minute fire-rated lock (SS 332 / EN 1634-1). Apply this to most HDB corridor flats.
Can a locksmith open a digital lock without drilling?
Most digital lock lockouts (Samsung, Yale, Gateman) are caused by battery or firmware issues and can be resolved non-destructively. If the lock is jammed mechanically, drilling may be needed ($150+).
I'm locked out at midnight — how fast can someone reach me?
Average response is 20–30 minutes island-wide if the locksmith has a nearby team. Remote areas like Tuas or Jurong Island may take 40–50 min and carry a $20–$30 travel surcharge.
Do I need to prove I live here before a locksmith opens my door?
Reputable locksmiths will ask for ID or Singpass verification to prevent unauthorised access. Have your IC or a photo of your utility bill/tenancy agreement ready.
Is it cheaper to send a WhatsApp photo of my lock before booking?
Yes — most legitimate locksmiths will give a firm quote via WhatsApp if you send a clear photo. This also ensures they bring the right parts, avoiding repeat visits.
My HDB letterbox lock is broken — can a locksmith fix it?
Yes, letterbox lock replacement costs $35–$95 depending on the lock type. Most locksmiths handle this as a quick job under 15 minutes.
