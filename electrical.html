How much does an electrician cost in Singapore?

Simple fixes like socket replacement start from $50–$80. Power trip diagnosis and repair ranges from $80–$200.

Can I fix a power trip myself?

No. It's dangerous and potentially illegal in Singapore. Call a licensed electrician to diagnose and fix safely.

What is an EMA Licensed Electrician?

In Singapore, anyone doing electrical work must be licensed by the Energy Market Authority (EMA). Our network has certified Electricians (LEW).
