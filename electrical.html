Electrical FAQs

How much does an electrician cost in Singapore?

Simple fixes like socket replacement start from $50–$80. Power trip diagnosis and repair ranges from $80–$200. New wiring jobs depend on complexity.

Can I fix a power trip myself?

No. If you're not licensed, it's dangerous and illegal in Singapore. Call a licensed electrician to diagnose and fix the root cause.

What is an EMA Licensed Electrician?

In Singapore, anyone doing electrical work must be licensed by the Energy Market Authority (EMA). Our network has Electricians (LEW, LE, Licensed Worker).

Do you offer emergency electrician service?

Yes! Power trips and sparking outlets are emergencies. Many electricians on our network offer same-day or within-2-hours emergency response.
