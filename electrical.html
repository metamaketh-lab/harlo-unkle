Yes — all our electrical technicians are EMA-licensed (Energy Market Authority). Work is certified and insured.
My home keeps tripping — what's the cause?
Common causes: overloaded circuit, faulty appliance, worn wiring, or a tripping Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (ELCB). An electrician will diagnose and fix.
