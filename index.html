How much do services usually cost?

It depends on the job. Simple fixes like tap replacement start from $50–$80. Emergency callouts or major work can be $100–$300+. We recommend asking for a quote first — all our providers offer transparent pricing before starting work.

Can someone come today?

Yes! Many providers offer same-day service, especially for urgent issues like plumbing leaks, power trips, and door lock failures. For less urgent jobs, next-day is usually available.

Do you cover all Singapore areas?

Absolutely! From Jurong to Tampines, Woodlands to Pasir Ris — we have providers covering every HDB town, Condo, and Landed estate across Singapore.

How fast can I get a reply?

Most vendors reply within the hour via WhatsApp or phone. Just tap the "Call Unkle Now" button and you'll be routed to the nearest available provider.
