How We Choose Providers

Our Vetting Process

Every provider on our platform goes through a screening process before we recommend them. Here's what we look for:

✅

Licensed & Insured

All electrical providers must hold a valid EMA licence (Energy Market Authority). Plumbers understand PUB requirements for waterworks. Gas hob technicians are EMA-registered gas service workers. We verify this before onboarding.

⭐

Track Record

Providers must demonstrate at least 3 years of hands-on experience in their trade. For aircon servicing, this means familiarity with split systems, cassette units, and VRV/VRF systems common in Singapore's HDB and condo landscape.

💬

Transparent Pricing

No bait-and-switch. Providers must give a firm quote before starting work — whether via WhatsApp photo assessment or on-site visit. Hidden fees are the #1 complaint from Singapore homeowners, and we don't tolerate it.

🏠

HDB & Condo Familiarity

Our providers understand the unique requirements of Singapore housing: SCDF fire-rated door locks for corridor HDBs, HDB renovation permit awareness, MCST rules for condo maintenance work, and the difference between a 4-room and 5-room HDB toilet layout.

⏱

Response Time

Emergency services must be able to respond within 24 hours. For urgent call-outs (locked out, active leak, power trip), we prioritise providers who can reach you within 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on your location.

🔄

Ongoing Quality Monitoring

We don't just vet once and forget. We continuously monitor provider performance based on homeowner feedback. Providers who consistently underperform are removed from our network.
