FAQ

Common Questions

How much does TV wall mounting cost in Singapore?
TV mounting from $80 for screens up to 55". Larger TVs (65"–85") cost $120–$200 depending on bracket and wall type.
Can you mount on hollow / partition walls?
Yes — we use toggle bolts or find the studs. For heavy items, we may recommend a backing plate. Always tell us your wall type when booking.
Do you provide the wall mount bracket?
We can supply one or mount one you already have. If you bring your own, we just charge for installation.
Can you conceal the TV cables?
Yes — we use wall channels or drill through walls to hide cables behind the TV. From $30 extra.
Do you mount on HDB bomb shelter walls?
Bomb shelter walls are reinforced concrete. We can drill them but use heavy-duty anchors. There may be a small surcharge for heavy items.
