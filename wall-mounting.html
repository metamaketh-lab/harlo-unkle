Frequently Asked Questions — Wall Mounting Singapore

How much does wall mounting cost in Singapore?

Simple TV mounting starts from $40–$60. Heavy items (large shelves, heavy mirrors) range from $60–$120 depending on wall type and weight.

Can you mount TVs on all HDB wall types?

Yes. Whether it's a solid concrete wall, hollow partition, or drywall, our experts use the correct anchors (wall plugs, toggle bolts, or chemical anchors).

Do you provide the mounting bracket?

You can provide your own, or we can source one for you during the quote phase. We ensure the bracket is rated for your TV's weight and VESA pattern.

Is it safe to drill into HDB bomb shelter walls?

Special care is needed. We drill shallow holes into the plaster layer only, avoiding the structural concrete. Our experts know exactly how to do this safely.

How quickly can a mounting expert arrive?

Most can arrive within 24 hours. For urgent needs (e.g. you just bought a TV today), we can often arrange same-day service.
