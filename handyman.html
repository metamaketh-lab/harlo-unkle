FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a handyman cost in Singapore?
Most handymen charge $40–$80/hr with a 2-hour minimum. Small jobs like mounting a shelf cost around $40–$80 flat rate.
Do you assemble IKEA or online furniture?
Yes — IKEA, Court, Harvey Norman, Shopee, Lazada — all brands assembled. From $40 per item depending on complexity.
Can a handyman paint my HDB room?
Yes — full room painting from $150–$350 including prep, primer, and topcoats. Feature walls and ceiling touch-ups also available.
What's the minimum job size?
Most handymen have a 2-hour minimum ($80–$120). Small tasks like hanging a curtain rod or mounting a shelf can be done in one visit.
Do handymen handle electrical or plumbing work?
Basic tasks (light fixture swap, tap washer replacement) yes. For full rewiring or pipe work, we route you to a licensed electrician or plumber.
