Handyman FAQs

How much does a handyman cost in Singapore?

Typical handyman jobs start from $40–$80. Larger projects like room painting or full furniture assembly range from $80–$200.

Can the handyman come on short notice?

Most handymen can arrive within 24 hours. For urgent fixes, some are available same-day.

What types of work do handymen handle?

From furniture assembly to painting, light fixtures, curtain installation, and general home repairs. Small jobs are welcome!

Do I need to provide tools or materials?

Handymen on our network bring their own tools. If specific materials are needed (paint, hinges, etc.), they'll let you know during the quote phase.
