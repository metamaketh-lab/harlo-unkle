FAQ

Common Questions

My HDB toilet is choked — how much does it cost to unblock?
Basic toilet choke clearing starts at $80–$150. For HDB main stack blockages (multiple units affected), jetting costs $200–$500.
Do you do same-day plumbing in Singapore?
Yes — most providers offer same-day service, especially for leaks and chokes. Call before 10 AM for the best chance of a same-day visit.
My water heater isn't working — can it be repaired?
Most water heater issues (faulty element, thermostat, leaking tank) are repairable from $60–$250. If the tank itself is corroded, replacement from $250 is needed.
How do I know if I have a hidden pipe leak?
Signs include: water meter running when all taps are off, damp patches on walls/ceilings, sudden spike in water bill. Licensed plumbers use pressure testing to find hidden leaks.
Are your plumbers EMA-licensed for gas work?
Yes — all gas-related repairs (water heater gas line, cooker pipe) are handled by EMA-licensed technicians only.
