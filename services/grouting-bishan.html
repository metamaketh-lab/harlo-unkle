Epoxy Grouting FAQ

How much does epoxy grouting cost for a 4-room HDB in Bishan?
Full 4-room flat costs $2,000-$3,500 (~$3.50-$6 psf). Single bathroom from just $280. Providers serve all of Bishan and surrounding areas.
Can I apply epoxy over existing cement grout?
No -- old grout must be removed to 2-3mm depth first. Applying over existing grout causes adhesion failure.
Does epoxy really prevent mould in Singapore?
Yes -- epoxy is non-porous and virtually mould-resistant. Much better than cement grout in humid HDB bathrooms.
Do I need HDB or MCST approval?
No -- epoxy grouting is cosmetic maintenance, not hacking. No BCA/HDB permit required.
