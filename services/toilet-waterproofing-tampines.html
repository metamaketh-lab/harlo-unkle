FAQ

Toilet Waterproofing FAQ in Tampines

My HDB toilet ceiling leaks from above, who pays?
HDB Goodwill Repair Assistance (GRA) covers most costs — each owner capped at ~$350 per toilet (50/50 split). Top-floor leaks covered by Town Council.
What is PU injection and how does it work?
PU resin injected into concrete cracks through small holes. Expands and seals the leak. $300-$1,000. No hacking needed, done in 1-2 hours.
How do I know if I need no-hack or full hack?
Minor leaks: PU injection or flood infusion ($300-$1,500). Severe membrane failure: full hacking ($1,500-$4,000). Professional inspection determines which.
My neighbour refused to fix their leak, what next?
Under BMSMA, upper unit is liable. File with Strata Titles Board ($500 filing fee) or Small Claims Tribunal (up to $20,000).
