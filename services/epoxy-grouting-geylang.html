Epoxy Grouting FAQ in Geylang

How much does epoxy grouting cost for a 4-room HDB?
Full 4-room flat typically $2,000-$3,500 at $3.50-$6 per sqft. Single bathroom starts at $280-$850.
Does epoxy grout really prevent mould in Singapore humidity?
Yes, epoxy is non-porous and mould-resistant. Cement grout turns black in 1-2 years. Epoxy stays clean for 5+ years.
Can you epoxy over existing cement grout?
No, old cement grout must be cut out to 2-3mm depth first. Applying epoxy over cement leads to adhesion failure.
Does regrouting require HDB or MCST approval?
No, regrouting is cosmetic maintenance, not structural work. No BCA or HDB permit needed.
